Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools and materials:

  • Oil drain pan
  • Socket wrench set
  • Oil filter wrench
  • New oil filter
  • Fresh motor oil
  • Funnel
  • Jack and jack stands

Step 2: Prepare Your Vehicle

Park your car on a level surface and let the engine cool down for at least 30 minutes. This ensures the oil isn't too hot to handle safely.

Step 3: Drain the Old Oil

Locate the oil drain plug underneath your vehicle and carefully remove it with the appropriate socket wrench. Allow all the old oil to drain completely into your drain pan.
